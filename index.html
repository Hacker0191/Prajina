Prajina Lamichhane
Prajina is a Nepal born and raised musician who started her artistic career in 2021. Her first EP was titled "Nineteen" which included songs with the subject of mental health, the new gen teenage angst and the matter of hearts.
She later released an English single named "Don't have to" discussing self-love and self-reflection lyrically. Her two singles "Hami" and "Yo Sahar" that were written in her mother tongue gained her traction throughout her nation. The songs filled with lyrics and sound of longing and love pulled the heart strings of my listeners making it a turning point in her career. She recently released a new EP named "Shabda haru" quite literally translating to "words" filled with genres like fusion pop, UK garage, pop rock, etc sound wise. Her songs come with a level of relativity that makes them loved by so many.
